Types

GENUINE

Made from 100 percent genuine cow skin. Tanned to perfection.

SYNTHETIC

A very popular alternative to genuine leather, good quality and budget friendly.

FAUX SUEDE

A different kind of texture than the genuine and synthetic variants. Smooth finish and classy feel.

Design Option

EMBOSSED

The design is engraved on to the leather by the die-cast/mould.

EMBOSSED BURN

The design is engraved on to the leather with a dark burn effect inside the design.

EMBOSSED PRINTED

The design is engraved on to the material by the mould/die cast and then the embossed area is colored by ink.

CUT TO SHAPE BORDERS

Borders that can into regular shapes like circle, square, rectangle, as well as custom shapes according to the design.

SCREEN PRINTING

Partial or full CMYK screen printing on your leather patches. Can use up to 7 different colors.

SINGLE STITCH LINE

A simple stitch line which is worked around the leather patch.

DOUBLE STITCH LINE

A double stitch line which is around the patch for.

EMBROIDERY ON PATCH

The patch is inserted into embroidery machine so that your design can be embroidered onto it.

STITCHING CHANNEL

A debossed stitch channel is left around the patch for later sewing it on to articles or garments.

ZIG ZAG STITCH LINE

A zig zag moving stitch line around the border of the patch

X STITCH LINES

X shaped stitch line moving around the border of the patch

BACKINGS

SEW ON

Patches with plain sew on backing can be attached by sewing the patches to hats, shirts, bags or any other article.

IRON ON

The most popular backing if you want to permanently attach your patches to clothing, hats, bags or any article.

VELCRO

Hook and loop velcro backing is a convenient choice to attach your patches if you have more than one patch.

MAGNET

Magnet patches are great for attaching to metal surfaces.

PIN

A great choice for one off events when you want to temporarily attach a patch and remove it later when you are done.

STICKER

A great choice for attaching your patches to smooth surfaces and then removing them later on as and when required.

USES

DENIM

BOrder custom leather patches to be used on denim products like jackets and jeans.

JACKETS

Order custom leather patches for your leather jackets.

LABELS

Order custom leather labels to use it on jackets, bags and clothing articles.

HATS AND BEANIES

Order custom leather patches to be used on hats, caps and beanies.
