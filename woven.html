True Customization Features

If your design has a lot of detail and you are looking for a fabric patch, woven patches might be what you are looking for. Woven patches are stitched flatter and thinner then embroidered patches, sometimes embroidery cannot create the desired look if the design is very detailed and small, this technique will not let the design loose detail and gives it a perfect rich feel, very similar to clothing labels inside the clothes. Our woven patches come with various backing options to choose from. Our woven patches come in different material like cotton, satin, damask etc. which you can choose from or let us recommend you something depending on your budget or usage. A merrowed border is also a must have if you are ordering woven patches to keep the edges from fraying, as these patches are very thin a merrowed border will help maintain the shape.

BACKINGS

SEW ON

PVC patches with plain sew on backing can be attached by sewing the patches to hats, shirts, bags or any other article.

Iron On

The most popular backing if you want to permanently attach your patches to clothing, hats, bags or any article.

VELCRO

Hook and loop velcro backing is a convenient choice to attach your patches if you want to switch different patches.

MAGNET

Magnet patches are great for attaching to metal surfaces.

PIN

A great choice for one off events when you want to temporarily attach a patch and remove it later when you are done.

STICKER

A great choice for attaching your patches to smooth surfaces and then removing them later on as and when required.
